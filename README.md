**EV Analysis Dashboard

**Project Overview

The EV Analysis Dashboard is an interactive Power BI dashboard designed to analyze 
Electric Vehicle (EV) adoption trends, vehicle distribution, and market growth. The 
dashboard provides insights into EV registrations, manufacturer performance, vehicle types, 
and regional adoption patterns to support data-driven decision-making.

**Objectives

Analyze Electric Vehicle adoption trends.
Track total EV registrations over time.
Compare Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and Plug-in Hybrid Electric Vehicles (PHEVs).
Identify top-performing vehicle manufacturers and models.
Evaluate EV distribution across regions and years.

**Tools & Technologies

Power BI
Power Query
DAX
Microsoft Excel

**Data Preparation

Removed duplicate records.
Handled missing values.
Standardized data formats.
Validated data quality for accurate reporting.
Created calculated columns and measures using DAX.

**Dashboard Features

Total EV Registrations KPI
BEV vs PHEV Analysis
Vehicle Make & Model Analysis
EV Adoption Trend by Year
Geographic Distribution of EVs
Interactive Filters and Slicers
Dynamic Charts and Visualizations

**Key Insights

Identified growth trends in EV adoption.
Analyzed the market share of major EV manufacturers.
Compared adoption rates across regions.
Evaluated the popularity of different EV models.
Tracked year-over-year EV growth patterns.
